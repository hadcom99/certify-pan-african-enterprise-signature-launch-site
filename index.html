How it works

1

Upload and auto-detect fields

Drop a contract into Certify; AI-powered field detection identifies signature blocks, date fields, and initials with >97% accuracy in seconds. No manual form mapping required. For complex documents, one-click refinement lets legal teams adjust detection in real time.

2

Invite signers, collaborate live

Send signing links to all parties. Multi-party signers see live cursors as others review and sign in parallel—Figma-style real-time collaboration. Comments, redlines, and version history are native to the platform; no email pinging required.

3

Sign with QES authority

Every signature is immediately Qualified Electronic Signature (QES)—legally defensible under Algerian digital signature law and UEMOA directives. No premium upgrade, no per-envelope tax. Audit trail and certificate of completion are auto-generated and stored on Africa-resident servers.

4

Archive, integrate, compliance-ready

Signed documents are stored in Africa-resident data centers with full compliance metadata. Native API integrations with CIB banking rails and Algeria Post e-ID ecosystem; webhooks and Zapier connectors enable seamless workflow automation into existing enterprise systems.
